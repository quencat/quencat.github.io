I am an interdisciplinary designer, working at the intersection of physical computing, virtual reality, and interactive art.

I often explore the metaphors in the living, breathing, and growing qualities of humans, and I use creative writings to support my visual narrative. I work with sensors, sometimes collecting biosignals and gestures, to bridge the physical and the virtual.

I earned my MDes from the University of Illinois Chicago in 2023, with a thesis on interactive typographic compositions in the CAVE2TM immersive hybrid-reality space. Prior to that, I graduated from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign in 2019, with a BS in Psychology and Anthropology.

Currently, I am looking to work on long-term projects related to interactive art experiences and community building. If you are interested in collaborating, or have openings in your studio/laboratory, please email me at xenonbxy@gmail.com. I am always eager to learn more and improve my skills. Thank you very much!
