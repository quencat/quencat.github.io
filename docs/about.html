I am an interdisciplinary designer, working at the intersection of physical computing, virtual reality, and interactive typography.

I often explore the metaphors in the living, breathing, and growing qualities of humans, and I use creative writings to support my visual narrative.

I like working with sensors, collecting biosignals and gestures, to bridge the physical and the virtual.

Check out my thesis An Elegy for the Departed, my design for the CAVE2TM immersive hybrid-reality space.
