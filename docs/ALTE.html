ALTE

2021

ALTE is a single-player escape room game. The player must find items, clues, and keys to solve the puzzles while maintaining health, in order to survive.

The player must alternate between three identities, each with their advantages and limitations, to move forward in the three levels.

Collaborators: Shalin Patel, Ansar Khan
