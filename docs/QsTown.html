QsTown

2021

QsTown is a click and shoot game inspired by my concept of “home”.

The circular board is divided into four areas, and each area represents a season of my hometown from my memory. Each season has its own set of items. In spring, there are sunflowers and orchids, and you can play football with foxes. In summer, there is rain and arcade games, and you can throw pixelated onions to collect retro game items. In fall, you can bike through maple trees and throw leaves at the bushes. In winter, you can throw snowballs at the snowmen. Built in Unity.
